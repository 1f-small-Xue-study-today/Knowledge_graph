# Kinodynamic Planning

## Overview

Kinodynamic planning extends classical path planning by considering both kinematic constraints and dynamic feasibility. It searches for control inputs that drive the system from start to goal while satisfying dynamics and constraints.

## Techniques

- **Sampling‑based planners:** Algorithms like kinodynamic RRT and RRT* sample control inputs and integrate dynamics.
- **Trajectory optimization:** Use optimization to refine paths generated by sampling or to compute time‑optimal maneuvers.
- **Reachability analysis:** Compute reachable sets under actuator limits and integrate with collision checking.

Kinodynamic planners are essential for mobile robots and drones with dynamics limits.
