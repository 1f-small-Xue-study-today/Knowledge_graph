# 2026-W28 Short-Term Plan

## Scope

Prepare the next motor/gimbal control study block around disturbance observation and observer-based robust control.

## Disturbance Observer (DOB)

Status: future planned.

Goals:

- Review the DOB structure for motor and gimbal control loops.
- Define nominal plant model, inverse model, low-pass Q-filter, and disturbance estimate path.
- Compare DOB behavior against ESO/LADRC disturbance estimation.
- Identify implementation constraints for sampling rate, noise filtering, saturation, and phase lag.

Expected update targets:

- `topics/dob.md`
- `skills/design_disturbance_observer.md`
- `projects/advanced_motor_control_project.md`
- `projects/gimbal_disturbance_rejection_project.md`

## LADRC Observer

Status: future planned.

Goals:

- Review the LADRC observer/ESO formulation for motor speed, position, or gimbal attitude loops.
- Tune observer bandwidth relative to controller bandwidth and sensor-noise limits.
- Compare observer estimates against measured disturbance, current, speed, and attitude data where available.
- Document when LADRC observer design is preferable to classical DOB in the motor/gimbal workflow.

Expected update targets:

- `topics/ladrc.md`
- `topics/eso.md`
- `skills/design_eso.md`
- `skills/implement_ladrc_controller.md`
